Daily Habits

  1. Start your morning with 5 minutes of deep breathing and gratitude journaling.
  2. Fill half your plate with colorful veggies at each meal.
  3. Move for 30 minutes—walk, dance, stretch or cycle!
  4. Digital sunset: log off devices 60 minutes before bedtime.
  5. Connect: call a friend or share a meal with loved ones.
